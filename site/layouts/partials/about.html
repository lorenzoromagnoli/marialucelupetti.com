I am a designer and Postdoctoral researcher at AiTech, TU Delft, The Netherlands. My work is focused on the intersection between design, AI and robotics. I hold a PhD in "Production, Managment and Design" from Politecnico di Torino (Italy, 2018). Previous to this position, I was research fellow at Amsterdam Institute for Advanced Metropolitan Solutions (AMS) (2018-2019) and visiting research fellow at X-Studio, Academy of Art and Design, Tsinghua University, Beijing, China (2016-2017).
