I am an Assistant Professor in Design at the faculty of Industrial Design Engineering, TU Delft (NL), and member of the multi-faculty research initiative on Meaningful Human Control over Intelligent Autonomous Systems AiTech. My current research, at the intersection of design, ethics, AI and robotics, is focused on understanding and designing responsible human-technology relations. I hold a PhD in "Production, Managment and Design" from Politecnico di Torino (IT, 2018). Prior to this position, postdoctoral researcher at AiTech (NL, 2019-2021), research fellow at AMS institute (NL, 2018-2019) and visiting research fellow at X-Studio, Academy of Art and Design, Tsinghua University (CN, 2016-2017).
